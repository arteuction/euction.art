About This Goal

Peace, stability, human rights and effective governance based on the rule of law are important conduits for sustainable development. This goal seeks to significantly reduce all forms of violence, end abuse and exploitation, promote the rule of law, reduce illicit financial and arms flows, and ensure responsive, inclusive, participatory and representative decision-making.
