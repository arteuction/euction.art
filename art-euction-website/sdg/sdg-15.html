About This Goal

Life on land is being threatened at an unprecedented rate through human activities. Forests are being cut down, wildlife habitats destroyed and biodiversity lost. This goal focuses on managing forests sustainably, combating desertification, halting and reversing land degradation, halting biodiversity loss, and protecting threatened species.
