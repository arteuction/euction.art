Frequently Asked Questions

What are the selection criteria?

We look for artists with strong portfolios, clear alignment with SDG values, and a commitment to social impact through their work.

How much do artists earn?

Artists receive 20-25% of auction proceeds. 75-80% goes directly to NGO partners supporting your chosen SDG.

Can I submit physical or digital art?

We accept both! Physical artworks are photographed for the platform, while digital works can be minted as NFTs.

What happens after I apply?

Our curation team reviews applications within 5-7 days. If accepted, you'll receive onboarding materials and can start submitting artworks.
