About This Goal

Cities are hubs for ideas, commerce, culture, science, and social development. However, many challenges exist to maintaining cities in a way that continues to create jobs and prosperity while not straining land and resources. This goal seeks to make cities inclusive, safe, resilient and sustainable with focus on affordable housing and sustainable transport systems.
