Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education

About This Goal

Education enables upward socioeconomic mobility and is a key to escaping poverty. Education helps reduce inequalities and reach gender equality, and empowers people everywhere to live more healthy and sustainable lives. This goal seeks to ensure that all girls and boys complete free primary and secondary schooling by 2030, aiming to provide equal access to affordable vocational training, eliminate gender and wealth disparities, and achieve universal access to quality higher education.
