About This Goal

Energy is central to nearly every major challenge and opportunity. Ensuring universal access to affordable, reliable, sustainable and modern energy is essential. This goal aims to substantially increase the share of renewable energy in the global energy mix and double the global rate of improvement in energy efficiency by 2030.
