How It Works

The complete journey from art to impact

1

Artists Submit

Artists from around the world submit artworks aligned with specific SDGs, sharing their vision for change and the causes they're passionate about.

  • Choose an SDG that resonates
  • Upload high-quality artwork images
  • Share your story and impact vision
2

Curated Selection

Our expert curation team reviews submissions to ensure quality and authentic alignment with SDG missions.

  • Quality and authenticity verification
  • SDG alignment assessment
  • Artist background verification
3

Transparent Euctions

Selected artworks are featured in themed euctions, with all transactions verified on blockchain for complete transparency.

  • Blockchain-verified bidding
  • Real-time auction updates
  • Secure payment processing
4

Direct Impact

Proceeds are distributed directly to verified NGOs working on the specific SDG, with full transparency and impact tracking.

  • 75-80% goes directly to NGOs
  • Real-time impact tracking via SIA
  • Quarterly impact reports

Real Impact, Real Change

Since our launch, ART EUction has facilitated over $1.2M in direct funding to verified NGOs working on all 17 SDGs. Every artwork sold creates a ripple effect of positive change.

From funding ocean cleanup initiatives to supporting education programs in underserved communities, our platform transforms artistic passion into tangible global impact.
